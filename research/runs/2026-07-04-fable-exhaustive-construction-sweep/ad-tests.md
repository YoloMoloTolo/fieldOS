## C09 — Working capital (audience: subs 1-4 yrs, commercial)

   handing us the biggest contract we'd ever seen. How does anyone survive year two?"
2. "Factoring wanted 15% of receipts. Retention already IS the profit on the job. What's left?"
3. "Second year in a row borrowing to make payroll in a profitable company. Is this just the
   business?"

## C12 — Estimating capacity (audience: owner-operators, fab/specialty)

   price them?"
5. "Anyone else's 'estimating department' just... you, at the kitchen table at 11pm?"
