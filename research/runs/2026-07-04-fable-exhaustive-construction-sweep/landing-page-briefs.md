# Landing Page Briefs — 2026-07-04 Sweep

Experiment 2 of the ladder. Build ONLY after the matching smoke test (ad-tests.md) produces
replies with specific recent pain. One page = one role + one workflow + one promise + one CTA.
CTA is "book a 20-min diagnostic" or "join pilot list" — never "buy." Forms collect: role,
trade, company size, the last time this happened, current workaround, urgency, budget range.
At least 20% of qualified signups must describe a specific recent event, or the page fails.

## Brief 1 — C12 "Bid Desk" (highest score)
- **Audience**: owner-operators of specialty subs/fab shops who self-estimate.
- **Promise**: "Send us the plans by 5pm. Get a takeoff and priced draft back before your
  coffee." (Concierge estimating — human-checked, not software-trial.)
- **Proof block**: mirror evidence language — kitchen-table-at-11pm, bids-not-sent.
- **CTA**: "Book a diagnostic: bring the bid you're about to skip."
- **Qualifying Qs**: bids/month, average bid value, who estimates today, last missed deadline,
  what outsourcing (if any) they've tried, monthly spend on it.
- **Kill criteria**: <2% qualified conversion or nobody brings a real plan set to the call.

## Brief 2 — C09 "Year-Two Capital" (needs a licensed partner before ANY offer)
- **Audience**: subs 1-4 years old taking first large GC contracts.
- **Promise (diagnostic-only)**: "Find out what your earned-but-unpaid position really is — and
  what it would take to get a bank to say yes." No lending is offered; this page tests whether
  the pain converts to a diagnostic conversation at all.
- **CTA**: "Book a free cash-position walkthrough."
- **Compliance note**: no credit terms, no rate claims, nothing that constitutes a loan offer.
  If commitment signals appear, next step is partnering with/being referred by licensed lenders
  (Billd-class), not building one.
- **Kill criteria**: subs won't share AR/contract detail on a call (trust wall = no product).

## Brief 3 — C08 "Punch Shield" (Eric's home trade)
- **Audience**: drywall/paint/trim/flooring sub owners on commercial or multifamily.
- **Promise**: "Walk off at substantial completion with evidence that ends the whose-damage
  argument." (Manual pilot: we run the completion-evidence walk on one job.)
- **CTA**: "Put one job in the pilot."
- **Qualifying Qs**: punch hours eaten last job, current photo habit, CompanyCam usage (if they
  use and love CompanyCam, record it — that's disconfirming data, not a lead).
- **Kill criteria**: pilots accepted but foremen won't execute the capture protocol, or GCs
  reject the evidence in the first real dispute.

Sequencing: run Brief 1 first (cheapest to fulfill manually, clearest paid substitute already
in market). Brief 2 only if C09 smoke tests outperform C12. Brief 3 rides on Eric's trade
credibility and can run parallel with in-network (non-gate-counting) dry runs.
