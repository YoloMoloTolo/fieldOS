# Top 15 Interview Directions — 2026-07-04 Sweep

Mom Test rules apply: past events only, no idea-pitching, hunt for money/time already spent.
Each direction lists WHO to recruit and the opening story prompt. Detailed per-opportunity
question sets live in opportunities.md.

1. **Subs 1-4 years in who hit the working-capital wall (C09)** — recruit via r/Concrete-type
   communities, local trade suppliers, GC referrals. "Tell me about the last job you almost
   couldn't make payroll on."
2. **Owner-operators who estimate after dinner (C12/C22)** — trades adjacent to Eric's network.
   "Walk me through last night after you got home from the site."
3. **Sub office managers on commercial work with 60+ day AR (C02)** — "Pull up your AR aging;
   tell me the story of the oldest one."
4. **Finish subs (drywall/paint/trim) on multi-punch projects (C08)** — Eric's own sub network.
   "How many punch hours did you eat on the last job?"
5. **Contractors who currently pay for outsourced estimating (C12)** — found in r/estimators
   threads; also the offshore estimators themselves (supply side). "What made you first send an
   estimate outside?"
6. **GCs who advance money to young subs (C09/R053)** — "Tell me about the last sub you fronted
   payroll for. What paperwork existed?"
7. **Homeowners 6+ months into a build that's over budget (C07)** — r/Homebuilding recruits.
   "Which allowance blew up first, and when did you realize?"
8. **Reno-loan lenders / HUD 203k consultants (C16)** — "What share of your reno loans stall
   mid-draw, and what does one cost you?"
9. **PM maintenance coordinators losing vendors (C14)** — "Which trade refused you most
   recently, and what did that emergency end up costing?"
10. **Vendors who quit PM work (C14, vendor side)** — "When did you stop taking PM calls, and
    what was the final straw invoice?"
11. **Subs with retainage currently frozen >90 days (C01)** — "Where is your oldest retainage
    dollar right now, and what have you done about it?"
12. **GC PMs who fought a CO price in the last 60 days (C05)** — "Show me the CO. How did you
    decide it was inflated, and how did it end?"
13. **Estimators at subs on bid-window trends (C11 — verification direction)** — collect actual
    ITB dates from inboxes: issued vs due, 2024 vs 2026. This is a data-pull interview to
    confirm or kill the compression claim.
14. **Owners who closed or nearly closed a healthy trade business (C22)** — "What was the week
    you decided (or almost decided) to shut it down?"
15. **Public-works subs on certified payroll (C24 — thin-cluster check)** — "Walk me through
    your last prevailing-wage audit or close call."

Recruiting note: directions 1-6 and 11-12 can start inside Eric's non-affiliated network within
TN (license-board lists, supplier counter contacts, GC sub lists) — but affiliated contacts
(Trim Design by N&R / LGC International's own clients and crews) do NOT count toward gates.
